Easy to use

 

Intuitive Control Panel

 

Manage backups easily via our web-based control console, software client or mobile app (iOS or Android).

 

Reliability and Access

 

Near continuous data protection

 

Our solution automatically detects and backs up new and changed files. 

 

 

Automatic or scheduled backups 

Tell TPS Backup when to perform your backups.

  • Automatic is the simplest method in which backups are performed while your computer is not in use.

  • Scheduled backups can be performed daily or weekly at a specified time of day.

 

Block-level incremental backups 

After the initial backup, TPS Backup only backs up new or changed portions of files, which saves bandwidth and ensures future backups are lightning fast.

Bandwidth throttling

Users can determine how much bandwidth they want to dedicate to their backups so other high-priority services can run unimpeded.

 

Security

Encryption

All user data is encrypted in-transit and at rest with military-grade encryption prior to transfer via a 128-bit SSL, 256-bit AES or 448-bit Blowfish connection. Users can choose a managed encryption key or a personal key for added security.

Data centers

All data centers employ state-of-the-art security, meet SAS70 standards, are SSAE 16 audited and ISO 27001 certified, and where applicable, adhere to European Union Safe Harbor Privacy Principles.

Reports

Administrators may set up and run reports regarding the backup health of their account that will be sent automatically to their email address on a daily, weekly, or monthly basis.

What We Support

Version (snapshot) support

Restore file versions up to 30 days in the past.

Back up computers, servers, and Network Attached Storage (NAS) appliances

Our solution backs up laptops, desktops, NAS, and mission-critical data on multiple servers including SQL, Exchange, Sharepoint and other file servers.

Windows and Mac support

We support Windows 7, 2008, Vista, XP, 2003, & 2000 (including servers) and Mac OS X 10.6, 10.5, & 10.4 (including servers).

Open and locked file support

MozyPro backs up all open and locked files, including Outlook PST files.
